Pork "secret"

Description

Known in Spanish as "secreto de cerdo", this part of the pig is delicious and doesn't necessarily involve making a long preparation. It has no bones, unlike other parts, and it's a bit expensive, but worth it.

Ingredients

  • Secreto de cerdo
  • Garlic, salt

Steps

  1. Buy the meat.
  2. At home, heat the frying pan. You can season the meat any way you want.
  3. Once the frying pan is hot, put there the meat. Fire should not be very intense, for it takes time to prepare the meat.
  4. Serve and eat.
