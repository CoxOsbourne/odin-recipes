Chicken breast

Description

The breast is a part of the chicken. It's very easy to prepare. For this recipe, it needs to be cut in steaks.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • "Papierosy" (chicken breast seasoning sold in Polish supermarkets)

Steps

  1. Buy the meat.
  2. At home, heat the frying pan.
  3. Once the frying pan is hot, put there the meat, wrapped up inside the seasoning envelope. Turn around after some minutes. Done and done.
  4. Serve and eat.
