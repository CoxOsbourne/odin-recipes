Burger with vegetables

Description

Just a burger with some sauteed vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Your favourite burger
  • Sauteed vegetables

Steps

  1. Buy the meat and vegetables
  2. (Optional) Prepare the vegetables, cutting them, etc.
  3. At home, heat the frying pan. You can season the meat any way you want.
  4. Once the frying pan is hot, put there the meat and vegetables.
  5. Serve and eat.
